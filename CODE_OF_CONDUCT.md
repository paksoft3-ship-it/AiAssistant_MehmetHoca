# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want EidosUs to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good intent; give and accept feedback graciously.
- Focus on what is best for users — especially students, researchers, and
  people who rely on accessibility features.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **hilalahmad.civilengineer@gmail.com**. Maintainers may
remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, and may ban
repeat or severe offenders.

This document is adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
